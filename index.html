About the Owner

Hello! I'm Meredith! Im currently living in Arizona studying at ASU. I have always been on the creative side, and I LOVE sharing my whimsy creations and ideas with people. Thank you for stopping by my shop and enjoy the sparkly and shiny treasures that take hold.

Our Story

Whimsical Treasures was founded in April 2024 after Meredith found inspiration from leftover beads she found tucked away in a closet. She started making necklaces and bracelets for her friends and loved ones. Other people started to notice the jewelry designs and said that Meredith should start her own jewelry business, which is exactly what she did!

What we Sell

Our necklaces and jewelry are affordable and customizable to whatever the customer wants. Below are the price ranges for different pieces of jewelry...
